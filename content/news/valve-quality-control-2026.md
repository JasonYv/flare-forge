## New Quality Standards

We are excited to announce **upgraded quality control procedures** across our entire valve manufacturing line. Every unit now undergoes three independent inspection stages.

## Inspection Process

![AI-powered valve inspection station](./valve-inspection.png)

Each valve is tested for:

- Pressure tolerance up to **4000 PSI**
- Dimensional accuracy within **±0.02mm**
- Surface finish quality (Ra < 0.8μm)
- Thread integrity and torque resistance

> Our new AI-powered inspection system catches defects that were previously invisible to manual inspection.

## Packaging Standards

![Anti-corrosion packaging](./valve-packaging.png)

All valves ship with:

1. Individual VCI (vapor corrosion inhibitor) wrap
2. Foam-lined cartons with shock indicators
3. Moisture-resistant shrink film
4. Digital inspection certificate via QR code
