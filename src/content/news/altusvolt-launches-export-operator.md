## A new bridge between Chinese manufacturing depth and global export markets

For over thirty years, the Lingyun battery factory in Baoji, Shaanxi has supplied first-fit batteries to a who's who of Chinese commercial vehicle manufacturers — Shaanxi Auto Group, Beiben Heavy Truck, Foton Auman, Sany Heavy Industry, Liugong, and CIMC. Today we're announcing **AltusVolt**: a dedicated global export operator that makes that same OEM-grade manufacturing platform directly accessible to international distributors, fleet operators, and brand owners.

### Why launch now

Three trends in the global lead-acid battery market converged to make this the right moment:

1. **Tier-1 European brands have consolidated supply chains**, leaving mid-tier distributors looking for new factory partnerships with audited quality systems
2. **AGM start-stop adoption is accelerating** in emerging markets that were previously dominated by conventional flooded — and few Chinese factories have IATF 16949 certification at this scale
3. **Off-grid solar storage demand in Africa and Southeast Asia** continues to outstrip supply, creating an opening for VRLA gel batteries built specifically for tropical duty cycles

### What AltusVolt offers that traditional trading houses can't

- **Single-source manufacturing** — no middleman trading, no quality drift between batches
- **Factory tours arranged for serious buyers** — Baoji is a 1-hour flight from Beijing
- **English-speaking technical sales** — application engineering, not just order taking
- **Full hazmat documentation** — UN2794, MSDS, IMDG declarations on every shipment
- **OEM/ODM partnership programs** — custom labeling, custom packaging, and tooling development
- **CCS type approval for marine** — one of very few Chinese batteries accepted on classed vessels

### Initial product line

The first products available under the AltusVolt brand cover seven Lingyun-manufactured categories:

- AGM Start-Stop (5 models, 60–105Ah)
- EFB Start-Stop (5 models)
- Maintenance-Free calcium-calcium (Japanese aftermarket sizes)
- Conventional flooded SLI (truck and industrial)
- Heavy-duty commercial truck (105–200Ah, OEM-validated)
- Solar off-grid VRLA (38–200Ah)
- Marine CCS-certified

A LiFePO4 lithium starter line is in development for Q3 2026 launch through partner facilities.
