## The World's Most Standardized Aftermarket Battery

The AV-MF-55D23 (Lingyun **55D23-MF** Bestar series) covers what is arguably the largest single battery fitment in the global aftermarket: the JIS D23 case for Japanese and Korean compact-to-midsize vehicles.

### Why JIS D23 Volume Matters

Toyota alone sold 9.5 million vehicles in 2024 — most fitted with a D23-class battery as OEM. Add Honda, Nissan, Mazda, Hyundai, and Kia and you have a global parc of 100+ million vehicles needing a D23 replacement every 3–5 years.

For aftermarket distributors and workshops, **stocking a single D23 SKU** covers replacements for dozens of vehicle models — minimizing inventory complexity while maximizing turnover.

### L+ vs R+

Always verify terminal layout before ordering:
- **55D23L** — Negative terminal on left when viewed from front
- **55D23R** — Positive terminal on left (less common)

Mismatched layout can cause cable strain, terminal damage, or short circuits against the hood.
