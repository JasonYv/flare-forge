## Built for Trucks That Sleep on Their Own Power

The AV-PAC-S490 (Lingyun **Obsidian S490 Pro**) addresses a fast-growing market: long-haul trucks fitted with electric parking air-conditioners. These systems draw 30–50A overnight from a battery bank that must crank a cold diesel engine the next morning — a duty cycle that destroys conventional starting batteries within 6 months.

### The Parking AC Problem

Modern long-haul trucks (especially in China, Russia, Middle East, North America) increasingly use electric parking air-conditioners to comply with anti-idling regulations. A typical parking AC consumes:

- **30–45 amps** for 8–10 hours (≈ 300–450 amp-hours per night)
- That's a **35% deep discharge** of a 1000Ah bank, every single night

Standard SLI batteries are designed for 5% discharges. Use them for parking AC and they'll be dead in 90 days. **You need a dual-purpose battery** that can:
1. Survive 600+ deep cycles
2. Still deliver 1000+ CCA at -18°C the next morning

That's exactly what the Obsidian Pro platform was designed for.
