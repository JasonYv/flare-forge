## The Smart Middle Path Between Flooded and AGM

The AV-EFB-70 (Lingyun **6-QTPE-70(700)**) addresses a critical market gap: vehicles equipped with basic start-stop systems where AGM is over-specified, but conventional flooded batteries fail prematurely.

Enhanced Flooded Battery (EFB) technology adds carbon and graphite to the negative active material, dramatically improving partial-state-of-charge (PSoC) tolerance. The result is **double the cycle life** of conventional flooded batteries at roughly half the cost premium of AGM.

### Where EFB Wins

- **Entry-level start-stop** without regenerative braking
- **Tropical climates** where AGM may suffer from heat-induced grid corrosion
- **Cost-sensitive markets** in Latin America, India, Southeast Asia
- **Fleet replacements** where AGM TCO is hard to justify
