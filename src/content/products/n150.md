## The Workhorse That Never Quits

The AV-SLI-N150 (Lingyun **N150** Feiyue series) is a traditional flooded JIS N-standard battery built for markets where simplicity, serviceability, and lowest cost-per-month-of-service matter more than maintenance-free convenience.

### Why Conventional Still Sells in 2026

In African, Central Asian, and Middle Eastern fleet operations, batteries don't get retired — they get serviced. A flooded battery with vent plugs can be:

- **Topped up** with distilled water every 3–6 months
- **Equalized** with a controlled overcharge to dissolve sulfation
- **Tested** with a hydrometer to identify a single weak cell
- **Saved** by replacing electrolyte after a deep discharge event

That field-serviceability extends practical life from 18 months (sealed MF) to 36+ months in regions with skilled mechanics and harsh duty cycles.
