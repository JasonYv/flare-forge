## Built for the World's Toughest Trucks

The AV-HD-150 (Lingyun **6-QW-150(800)**) is the export version of a battery validated by China's largest commercial vehicle OEMs — Shaanxi Auto Group, Beiben Heavy Truck, Foton Auman, Sany Heavy Industry, Liugong, and CIMC.

After three decades of supplying first-fit batteries to fleets that operate 18+ hours per day across mountains, deserts, and extreme temperatures, this platform has earned a reputation for one thing: **showing up to work, every day, for years.**

### What Makes Heavy-Duty Different

Long-haul trucks are not passenger cars with bigger engines. The duty cycle is fundamentally different:

- **Sustained vibration** at frequencies that destroy automotive plates
- **Sleeper cab parasitic loads** (TV, microwave, phone charging) overnight
- **Cold-weather starts** of 12L+ diesel engines drawing 700+ amps
- **Engine compartment temperatures** that age batteries 2× faster

The 6-QW platform addresses every one of these with extra-thick plates, calcium-alloyed grids, and a triple-anchor hold-down system.
