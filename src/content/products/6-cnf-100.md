## Powering Off-Grid Africa, One Home at a Time

The AV-SOL-100 (Lingyun **6-CNF-100**) is the workhorse of off-grid solar storage — a 100Ah 12V VRLA gel battery engineered for the punishing duty cycle of equatorial solar home systems.

### Why Lead-Acid Still Wins in Solar

Despite lithium's rise, lead-acid VRLA still dominates entry-level off-grid solar in 2026 because:

1. **Cost per watt-hour:** $0.18 vs $0.32 for lithium at retail
2. **No BMS required** — simpler installations, lower failure points
3. **Tolerates abuse** — survives over-discharge that would brick a lithium bank
4. **Recyclable supply chain** — 99% recycled in target markets
5. **No fire risk** — passes insurance underwriting easily

For the 600 million people who gained solar access between 2015 and 2025, lead-acid was the chemistry that made it affordable. The 6-CNF platform is purpose-built for that customer.
