## Engineered for Modern Start-Stop Vehicles

The AV-AGM-70 (Lingyun **6-QTF-70(720)**) is purpose-built for vehicles equipped with advanced Start-Stop systems and regenerative braking. Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) chemistry delivers superior cycling endurance, rapid charge acceptance, and uncompromised cranking power under extreme conditions.

### Why AGM for Start-Stop?

In a typical start-stop vehicle, the battery experiences 5–10× more discharge-recharge cycles than in a conventional vehicle. Standard flooded batteries fail within 18 months in this duty cycle. AGM technology immobilizes the electrolyte in absorbent fiberglass mats, allowing the battery to:

- Sustain partial state of charge (PSoC) operation indefinitely
- Accept regenerative braking energy without gassing
- Survive deep discharges that would destroy a flooded battery
- Operate spill-proof at any orientation

### Manufacturing Heritage

Produced in our IATF 16949-certified facility on European production lines (SOVEMA Italy, OBLF Germany), every AV-AGM-70 undergoes a 7-stage quality assurance process including computerized formation, leak testing, and high-rate discharge verification.
